Reason Behind Chatty

Every skill based worker needs a portfolio that they can show off as to prove their skills. As a web development student, I have always worried about the outside market. I do not have a complete confidence in my skills and I do not know how much I know.

Chatty will work as a proof for my skills and also as something that I can show off to other people. This is a time taking project but I have people who have been guiding me and supporting me. This is not just my hardwork. There is other people's support behind this project

Chatty is a portfolio. This is in no way a commercial product on which I would want to earn on. This is a learning project that I will grow as time goes on. It will nourish as I learn more and more.
